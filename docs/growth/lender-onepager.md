## Your borrowers' risk is your risk

When a borrower misses a crop-insurance deadline, under-documents a hail
claim, or panic-sells below breakeven in a bad week, the damage lands on
your loan book: weaker repayment capacity, more carryover requests, more
workouts. Every ag lender knows this; almost none have a tool that changes
borrower behavior between farm visits.

## What Neumeric does

A farm platform your borrowers actually use, built for the farmer's side of
the table:

- **Deadline compliance** — every RMA/FSA date for their crops and county,
  with reminders and consequences stated. The 72-hour notice-of-loss clock
  is tracked per damage event.
- **Claim evidence** — satellite change-detection plus guided phone photos,
  hashed and timestamped into an adjuster-legible packet. The farmer walks
  into a loss adjustment with documentation instead of a memory.
- **Program money** — ARC/PLC, storage-facility loans, conservation
  programs, disaster assistance matched to the operation, with the "why"
  shown. Found money improves debt-service coverage directly.
- **Marketing discipline** — the borrower's own position (breakeven, basis
  range, storage carry, insurance floor) and scenario math across price
  ranges, plus pre-committed targets they set themselves. Strictly
  decision-support: no price predictions, no trade recommendations — which
  means no CTA registration exposure for you or us to explain.

## Why a lender should care about the *evidence* part

Our records are engineered for auditability: append-only, hash-chained,
with satellite scenes and methodology versions pinned so any analysis can be
independently recomputed. When a borrower documents a loss through
Neumeric, the paper trail holds up — for the insurer, and for your file.
