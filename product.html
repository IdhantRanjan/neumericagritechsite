The product

One camera.
Three ways it pays.

The camera establishes ground truth about a physical asset. A financial product turns that truth into money for the farmer. Same core system — three applications.

01 · Building now

The insurance advocate

Farmers must report acreage, document yield history, and file claims after hail, flood, drought, or disease — a manual, error-prone process where the adjuster works for the insurer, not you.

Neumeric uses computer vision on drone and phone imagery to document damage objectively, build a claim that's fast and hard to deny, and catch the USDA/FSA program money that paperwork usually buries. Think TurboTax for crop insurance — found money, on your side.

Live focus
02 · Coming next

Parametric payouts

Traditional insurance pays only after a human adjuster inspects the damage — slow, and easy to dispute. Parametric insurance pays automatically when an objective trigger is hit.

Neumeric's vision system can be that trigger: a policy that pays out the moment drone imagery confirms stand loss past a threshold. No adjuster, no dispute, no paperwork — the same data underwrites the policy and releases the money.

Expansion
03 · Coming next

The marketing copilot

Farmers decide when and how much of the crop to sell across an 18-month window — juggling futures, local basis, storage cost, cash flow, and their insurance floor. Mostly on gut feel, which leads to panic-selling and missed value.

We don't predict prices — nobody honestly can, and it isn't our place to. We take your CV-verified yield and show your real position, model the scenarios, and help you sell with discipline instead of emotion.
