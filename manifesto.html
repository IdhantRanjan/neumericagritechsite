Our manifesto

The ground already tells the truth.

Someone should be listening for the farmer.

For a hundred years, the person who worked the land knew it better than anyone. Then the tools got expensive, the models got proprietary, and the truth about a field became something you had to argue for — against people with better cameras than you.

We believe the farmer should hold the clearest picture of their own ground. Not the insurer. Not the buyer. The person who planted it.

Technology in agriculture keeps arriving pointed the wrong way — at squeezing the grower, automating them out, or selling them a machine. We're not interested in that. The most valuable thing you can hand a farmer isn't a robot. It's certainty, and the leverage that comes with it.

  • 01Truth belongs to the ground, not the paperwork. The field is the record. Our job is to read it honestly and let it speak — no spin, no inflation, no theater.
  • 02Every tool we build takes the farmer's side. If the same technology can be aimed at the grower or for them, we aim it for them. Always.
  • 03We measure in outcomes, not features. A dashboard is not a result. A paid claim, a payout that hit the account, a sale made on purpose — those are results.
  • 04We stay honest about what's real. We won't predict prices we can't predict or promise a payout we can't back. Trust is the whole product.
  • 05The land outlasts the hype. We're building something a working farmer can rely on for decades — quiet, grounded, and worth the trust it asks for.

We're not here to disrupt farming. Farming doesn't need disrupting — it needs someone in its corner with the same tools everyone else already brought to the table.

— The Neumeric team
